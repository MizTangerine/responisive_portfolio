About Me

Hi! I’m Clarissa. My pronouns are she/her/hers. I HATE having my picture taken! I am from Southern Utah, a place so small you’ve probably never heard of it, called Spry. I grew up on a ranch, where I learned to love horses, problem solve, and work hard. I’m a mass appraiser by trade. Which means I appraise a lot of properties at once, for property taxes (yep, I’m the dreaded tax man). I love that I get to work with large amounts of data, and GIS is the toy that keeps me going back to the office. The process of learning to write SQL queries has been tough, but amazingly rewarding. I’m excited to learn more about coding, so I can put it to use at work, and possibly volunteering for a non-profit.

My two dogs, Lottie and Tanna, are the light of my life; literally the reason I get out of bed in the morning. They are both mutts, that were rejected from working on the ranch. In my spare time I like to read, currently my favorite genre is Historical Fiction. I also like to joke that I’m a hooker, someone who crochets, I also knit. I make lots of hats, baby bibs and blankets, toys, and bags as gifts. From time to time I enjoy spending time with my nieces and nephews, whom I collectively call ‘The Grandkids’.
